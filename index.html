About Us

Vision6 is an e-learning platform dedicated to empowering students through high-quality, interactive, and personalized learning experiences. By bridging the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world application, Vision6 helps students develop the skills, confidence, and understanding needed to achieve academic excellence and future success.


Introduction

Develops an application equipped with various interactive features, such as learning videos, quizzes, and digital modules for STPM students. This enables students to actively engage in learning and participate more effectively in the educational process." Alternatively, for a more formal project description: "This project aims to develop an application equipped with various interactive features, including learning videos, quizzes, and digital modules for STPM students. The application enhances student engagement by promoting active learning and encouraging participation throughout the learning process.


Mission

To provide accessible, engaging, and innovative e-learning experiences that help students achieve academic and professional success.


Vision

To become a leading digital learning platform that transforms education through technology and personalized learning.

Our Services

Tutorial videos

  • Providing professional e-teachers who are able to teach with a via pre- recording videos.
  • Lesson are designed by subject matter experts to align with specific curriculum standard.

Digital modules

  • Providing digital modules for all subject related to the latest curriculum standard
  • New updates will be notify.
  • Latest modules can be access easily due to collaboration with experts and government sector such as MAJLIS PEPERIKSAAN MALAYSIA (MPM)

Skill drills and Brain teaser

  • Focusing on practicing specific repetitive question.
  • Brain teaser/quizzes designed to reinforce the key concept covered in the videos and ensure student ready for the next module.
  • Progress saving

How It Work

Tutorial videos and live teaching

  • Student will need to watch the videos which has been provided by the teachers for the curriculums.
  • For some subject,an online live teaching will be provided,so that the subjects can be explained in more detail and student can do a QNA sessions with the live host.

Online Based

  • Student need to have an access to the internet,so that they can access to all of the data online.
  • New updates will be notify.

Digital modules 

  • Latest modules for all curriculums will be provided.
  • Students only need to read the modules that have been provided.​
